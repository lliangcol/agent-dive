# Comparisons

This catalog preserves comparative analyzes across projects, categories, or ecological levels. Comparison documents should be based on collected projects or clear sources, and do not replace single project deep dives.

## Comparison principle

- First explain the comparison dimensions, and then give the conclusion.
- Differentiate between learning value, engineering maturity and production availability.
- Do not use star counts as a substitute for actual analysis.
- Mark unverified items and dependent versions.
